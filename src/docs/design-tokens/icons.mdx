# Icons

    <p>
      Welcome to our Icon Gallery, a crucial part of our design system. Icons are more than just decorative elements; they play an essential role in enhancing user experience, conveying information quickly and efficiently, and maintaining a consistent aesthetic across our digital products.
    </p>
    <p>
      In our design system, each icon is implemented as an individual component. This approach offers several advantages:
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Reusability:</strong> Components can be reused across different parts of the application, ensuring consistency and reducing the need for redundant code.</li>
        <li><strong>Scalability:</strong> As our application grows, new icons can be added seamlessly as individual components, maintaining the system's modular nature.</li>
        <li><strong>Customization:</strong> Each icon component can be easily customized in terms of size, color, and other properties, allowing for flexibility while adhering to design guidelines.</li>
        <li><strong>Performance:</strong> By only importing the icons we need, we keep our application's bundle size smaller, which contributes to better performance.</li>
      </ul>
    </p>
    <p>
      Below is our curated collection of icons, each crafted to meet specific UI requirements while harmonizing with our overall design language.
    </p>
